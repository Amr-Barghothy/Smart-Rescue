About Smart Rescue

Empowering communities to report and respond to working at height emergencies with speed, precision, and care.

Our Mission

The Working at Height Association (WAHA) is dedicated to ensuring the safety of workers and individuals in elevated environments. Smart Rescue is our innovative digital platform that bridges the gap between those in need and specialized rescue authorities.

We believe that every second counts in emergency situations. Our platform leverages modern technology to streamline case reporting, coordinate volunteer responders, and provide real-time updates to ensure rapid and effective rescue operations.

What We Do


  • Emergency Response Coordination

    Connect citizens with specialized rescue authorities instantly


  • Volunteer Network

    Build a community of trained volunteers ready to assist


  • Precise Location Tracking

    GPS and map integration for accurate incident reporting
